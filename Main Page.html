How to Book a Ride

  1. Go to the website.
  2. Pick your pick-up station and destination.
  3. Check the fare using the calculator.
  4. If you’re a Senior, PWD, or Student, select your discount.
  5. Click confirm to book your ride.
  6. Wait for your motor or taxi to arrive.
